Home

Dani Lee arts exists to offer creative activities that develop, sustain and enrich communities.

Our projects are highly participatory. People of all ages and abilities are the co-creators. We inspire and enable people to make a creative contribution to their community and a positive change within themselves.
Dani Lee arts works collaboratively with communities to create stunning murals of mosaic or cob, earth ovens and sculpture.

We work in the heart of communities, streets, green spaces, schools, hospitals and other local spaces and have an excellent track record of work in London and Wales spanning 24 years.
