The communities we work with are at the heart of planning, designing and creating the artworks.

Dani Lee Arts facilitates and project manages the commissions. Participants to workshops receive inspiring teaching and can choose from an extensive range of stimulating materials.

School Garden Mosaic

School Garden Mosaic mural created by the children, their parents and teachers for their school vegetable garden.

Community Cob Mural

Mural created with the service users of Hafal, a charity that works with those that are experiencing mental health issues. This mural was created from cob, a fun and sticky mixture of sub-soil, sand, straw and water. In suitable locations this mural material lasts surprisingly well.

Click below for full image
  • Location - The Pensioners Hall, Ammanford
  • Funder - Education 2000

Playground Mosaic

Mosaic designed and made with the children at the school for their playground. Year 6 all made ceramic self portraits to put into the windows of the school building at the centre of the mosaic.

  • Location - Henry Cavendish Primary School, Streatham, London
  • Funder - School PTA
